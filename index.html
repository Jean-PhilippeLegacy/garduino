OUR ROOTS

In 2018, on a cloudy Monday afternoon, four students in the Interactive Media Specialist program at Fanshawe College where chatting about their weekend. When the topic of houseplants oddly came up, they all discovered there wasn’t a single greenthumb in thier collect eight thumbs. Each of them had stories of buying plants with the best of intentions, only to be throwing out the shrivled remains a few weeks later. Collectively they put their heads together, and using thier mixed Graphic Design and Coding backgrounds developed SQRoot. An app and sensor combination that would be their surrogate green thumbs.

The Product section

THE PRODUCT

SQRoot is a wireless plant sensor disguised as a rock that actually alerts you when your plant needs more sun light, fertilizer or water. Making sure you never forget about your plants again! This natural looking sensor takes away the guess work, letting you know what your plants needs and when it needs it! SQRoot has a database of hundreds of different plants, and can be used over and over again with all types of plants in your home.

HOW DOES IT WORK

SQRoot is a powerful sensor that can track your plants sun exposure, PH levels and soil moisture. When it connects with your device (phone or tablet) it is able to access a database of plant profiles, which it then uses to ensure your plant is recieving everything it needs, and in the quantity it needs it. SQRoot will then send you notifications when it is time to attend to your plant. It doesn’t matter how much experience you have caring for plants, the app will walk you through your plants routine, giving you step by step instructions along the way.
